Roles
- Master Admin
- Club Manager
- Coach (Head coad,Assistant coach, Team manager)
- Player/parent

what each role can do 
- Master Admin
    - he is the one owning the website
    - his job is to register multiple clubs and give all the further access to the club
    - he can have the details of the club
    - announcement sent by master admin is only visible to the Club Manager
    - he wont be able to add any teams or players it a spefic club
    
- CLub Manager
    - He is only one who can create the teams under his own club
    - sends invite link to all the coaches 
    - creates multiple teams
    - assigns coaches to the particular teams
    - same coach can play the role of head coach, assistant coach, and team manager
    - first action of the club manager is to invite coaches and have their details
    - can conducts tournaments/events within the club
    - manages the record of different teams in his clubs
    

- Coach
    - he cannot create any teams
    - he can add players to the team he is the coach of
    - has ability to remove or add players
    - all the duties in the particular team is handled by the coach
    - invites to players/parents is sent by the coach
    - announcement done by the coach is only visible to the players/parents invited to his team
    - maintsins all the trach of his team
    - cannot create any tournaments on his own
    - he can only add practice sessions
